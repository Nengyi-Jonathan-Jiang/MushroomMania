How to play

Make exotic mushrooms! Match three or more adjacent mushrooms to make a better mushroom. Watch out for truffle bombs!

Swipe left and right to select the column. Swipe up to rotate, swipe down to drop the mushrooms.

A and D to select the column. W to rotate, S to drop the mushrooms.
